R2R 2023

RECONNECT TO REST

R2R is a non-denominational 3-day conference hosted annually by Again and Afresh Church, Makurdi. It is a time dedicated to the worship of Christ by faithfully teaching His doctrine and having fellowship with His Spirit.

Register

R2R is a 3-Day Conference

Saints from all walks of life come together at R2R for soul-nourishing sessions of worship, prayers, and fellowship fueled by sincere love. Opportunity will be given for you to ask and receive answers to questions on matters of doctrine. Ministers will also be present who will, with gladness, communicate God's counsel on issues peculiar to the needs of your soul.
